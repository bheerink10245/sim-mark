Overview:
Six individuals have 6 unique "strategies" (simple side signals) that they use to create a  trade.
One Market maker uses Orderbook as API for the 6 individuals. Marketmaker has pricing strategy for each asset.
There are 10 tickers. Each ticker has its own fluctuation function every clk(some t-interval). Update price via some random change of some function.
Designate a "period" of trading 



S-Design
===============================================
All 7 individuals inherit from the Entity.hpp

Class Entity -> Class Market_Maker/Class Player.

All 10 tickers are objects of class Ticker.


Entity Characteristics
  -Log their trades/transactions      
  -Log their P&L                       
  -Check their capital                
  -Generate transaction 

Player Characteristics (inherits from Entity):
  -Generate a buy and sell signal
    -Implement their "strategy"
      -Strat 1: Buy/Sell for %3 decrease over 5 CLKs of Negative/Positive gain
      -Strat 2: 3 Green CLK buy
      -Strat 3: 3 Red CLKs buy
      -Strat 4: Buy once sell iff 5% profit margin
      -Strat 5: Inverse strat 
      -Start 6: If last 5 CLKs have all been negative
    -Monitor other players 


Maker Charactersitics (inherits from entity): 
  -Generate a spread margin
  -Control orderbook
  -Match orders between individuals




Ticker Characteristics:
Function:
  -void GeneratePriceFluctuation()
  -void TradeExecuted(Individual, Info)
